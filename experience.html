My Experience⁉

❇ I have many memories that I will never forget, which are visiting parliament, visiting Cameron Highlands, form 6 graduation, hang out with my friends. In 2019, my school paid a visit to parliament. I felt very happy and so excited because it was the first time I went to parliament. I can see the situation in parliament.After that, another experience that I will never forget is the trip to Cameron highlands with my friends. We went there for 3 days and 2 nights. We visit many places such as tea plantations, night markets, and others. It was a memorable moment for me.In addition, the best moments in my life were my graduation days!!!!!!!. I'm so excited because this was the day that I'm waiting for and this is also the last day I met my friends before going back to Kedah.
